You will be able to find this ballot by short 3-word memo or ballot SHA-256 hash on public bulletin board after it has been published via “Blockchain Explorer” button in “Monitor Elections”.

It is recommended to save these details at once at all times with button below to ensure encryption correctness and absence of fraud with which candidate is selected and which one is encrypted to the resulting ballot. You may delete these details later when the ballot has been published and just memorize or write down the 3-word memo.

Cryptographic SHA-256 hash here plays the key role in ensuring that current client cannot backdatingly change the candidate-option, chosen by you. As of contemporary technology, it’s impossible to replace the ballot within 10 years so that it matches the same hash.

This application cannot possibly know whether you’re going to decrypt the ballot obtained or to sign it.

If fraud is detected after the ballot is decrypted, it should be reported.
